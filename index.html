About Us

Entergaming is Juma Group’s state-of-the-art gaming platform provider, which grew out of over 10 years of proven industry success. Built with a modular approach, Entergaming services the retail betting and gaming industry.

All-In-One Back Office

The all-in-one back office is the third eye of the Entergaming platform, meaning it’s where operators get an insider’s view of the system’s backend. Operators are able to take advantage of a complete detailed reporting system that can be customised according to the needs of the operator.

Built-In Sports Betting Engine

The Entergaming betting engine is built on innovative architecture to support combinations of external source feeds with internally-calculated derivative markets. This results in an expandable set of varied market types that can differentiate operators from their competition.

Central Risk Management

Entergaming’s central risk management system includes real time calculation of liabilities, ‘what if’ scenarios, high stakes and negotiation scenarios, warning limits, and stop bet limits, all of which are displayed in real time. It also provides the ability to establish betting limits per player or per league based on maximum stakes or potential winnings.

More

Built-in Bonus Engine

The state-of-the-art bonus engine allows operators to create bonuses for all different channels, including automatic customer award calculations, and bonuses generated on deposits, turnovers, losses, as well as on specific sports, leagues, events, and markets.

Future Bets

This unique feature allows players to place future bets based on the potential winnings from other current bets. This brand new type of betting system, which is exclusive to Entergaming, will increase sales and the gross win of operators. It also provides players with a feature not available in mainstream betting operators.
