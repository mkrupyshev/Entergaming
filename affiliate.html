Affiliate System

- Affiliate Management Back-office to manage the affiliate setup, commercial programs, marketing material, monitoring and earnings distribution

- Affiliate website in order for affiliated sites to register & apply for affiliate deals, retrieve marketing material, monitor earnings, request payment, and communicate with operator.

- Fee calculation processes / commission schemes (configurable by the operator)

Fee calculations are set by the operator and are based on turnover, revenue share, and hybrid options. Additional to what is built in, the system can be connected to 3rd party affiliate systems through an API.
