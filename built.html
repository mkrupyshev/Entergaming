Built-In Sports Betting Engine

The Entergaming betting engine is built on innovative architecture to support combinations of external source feeds with internally-calculated derivative markets. This results in an expandable set of varied market types that can differentiate operators from their competition. The open architecture allows bookmakers to select a sport, tournament, event or market type to be handled as “feed automated” (all markets from the feed), as “fully automated” (all markets from the feed plus internal derivative markets), or as “manual” (main markets entered from bookmakers and sub-markets generated automatically).
