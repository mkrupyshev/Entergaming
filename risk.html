Central Risk Management

Entergaming’s central risk management system includes real time calculation of liabilities, ‘what if’ scenarios, high stakes and negotiation scenarios, warning limits, and stop bet limits, all of which are displayed in real time. It also provides the ability to establish betting limits per player or per league based on maximum stakes or potential winnings.

Limits and AI extensions
Limit values

The system supports setting limit values on the following data:
sales totals
liability totals on active bets
individual stake or liability limits on specific user or shop or user class (per bet, total per day/week or on specific tree configuration as below)

Limit value tree

Sales and liability totals can be applied in the following tree:
At operation levels (all sales channels or white labels)
At sales channel levels (white label or affiliate/agent network)
At sport, tournament, event, market type, forecast levels

Limit action types

Following actions are supported:
Block sales: Not allowing sales if a specific limit exceeds
Create warnings: displays a warning in the back office
Set a specific bet at Negotiation/High stakes status before accepting it: The related bets are displayed in real time high stake notifications and negotiation screens in the back office and on the user screens.

AI Algorithms

AI extension is a system module installed separately, allowing large scale operators to fully customize the risk management behavior using real time what if scenarios and automatically adjusted limits according to the total sales and player betting trends.
