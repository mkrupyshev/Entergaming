All-In-One Back Office

The all-in-one back office is the third eye of the Entergaming platform, meaning it’s where operators get an insider’s view of the system’s backend. Operators are able to take advantage of a complete detailed reporting system that can be customised according to the needs of the operator.

  • - Management for all sales channels and white labels
  • - Sports betting – Trading applications
  • - Live Betting Management
  • - Automatic odds generation setup
  • - Sports feed configuration
  • - Accounting / reporting
  • - Affiliate Management
  • - Retail store management
  • - Multi-currency operation and setup
  • - Built-in CRM & integrations with 3rd party CRM tools
  • - Built-in CMS
