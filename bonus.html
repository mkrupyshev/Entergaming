Built-in Bonus Engine

The state-of-the-art bonus engine allows operators to create bonuses for all different channels, including automatic customer award calculations, and bonuses generated on deposits, turnovers, losses, as well as on specific sports, leagues, events, and markets. Bonus types also include additional percentages of revenue based on the number of selected betting events.

Supported bonuses
  • - Standard internet bonuses (on first, next, total deposits)
  • - High odds bonuses (e.g. get additional 10% to play on a tournament or event)
  • - Flat amount bonuses on specific events or markets
  • - Free bet bonuses
  • - Bonus proportions for specific affiliate users
  • - Promotions per user group (bonuses for users of a sports fan club, etc.)
  • - Multi bet (higher payout % according to the number of events in a bet, scaled e.g. get 2% on doubles, 3% on triples â€¦ 10% if 10 events, etc.)
  • - Player classifications (e.g. all VIP players receive 10% better odds in sport A on Monday between 10:00 and 16:00)
  • - Batch bonuses and individual manual bonuses (competitions and drawings)
  • - Loyalty bonuses (e.g. spend/purchase an amount in the sports shop to receive a bonus)
  • - Jackpot type bonuses (combined with games or only sports, e.g. one sports bet each 10.000 gets a 100% bonus)
  • - Player specific bonuses (on birth/name days, etc.)
  • - Store, display and consume bonuses generated by third party systems (Casino, Poker, in house systems)
Additional unique options:
  • - Future bets (play from my future winnings, bets to be activated automatically if initial bet wins)
  • - Money back and Cash Out
  • - Player referrals (bring your friends to get bonuses)

The system supports setting limit values on the following data:
sales totals
liability totals on active bets
individual stake or liability limits on specific user or shop or user class (per bet, total per day/week or on specific tree configuration as below)
